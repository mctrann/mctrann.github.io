About Me

a typical bio

Hello, world! Here's a piece of me I leave to the internet. I come from the Midwest as a Vietnamese American second generation individual. I strive to understand the world through the lense of computational thinking, while helping others understand it too.

I am currently a student at UNL majoring in Computer Science with minors in Mathematics and Geography. Ideally, I will integrate these various subjects into a career that will enable me to help my community on a local and global scale.

My passions lie in community involvement whether tech related or not. I also enjoy helping people reach their fullest potential in their skillsets in order to strengthen a community. The goal is to bring together individuals of diverse backgrounds to strengthen communities and social networks. If we don't do it, who will?
